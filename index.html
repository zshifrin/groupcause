What is Bark N Brew?

Bark N Brew is an amazing event that brings loveable dogs and delicious beer together! Dogs and dog owners are welcome, as is anyone who might want to become a future dog owner! There will be dogs available for adoption and to play with for all of you beer lovers who also happen to be dog lovers. Check out our site and learn more about our events!
